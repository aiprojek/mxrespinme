in the MX respin ME project at various levels, from simply advising fellow users to becoming a core maintainer. Any contribution, even the smallest, is appreciated. We welcome everyone to improve MX respin ME and spread Free Sosftware!

Contribute to MX respin ME

Donate
Please be aware that the MX respin ME project is a non-profit, community-developed project or we can say that it is currently a personally developed project. That means we don't make any money. But what we do with MX respin ME takes time and we do it without anyone paying us even though we do it because we love it and want people to enjoy what we enjoy, a free and legal computer operating system.

On the other hand, in developing MX respin ME we also need resources such as information, ideas, ideas, or resources that require funding such as websites, electricity, equipment, and so on.

A Little Step

MX Respin ME is a computer operating system developed personally, based on the MX Linux operating system. MX Respin ME comes with Xfce, a stable, lightweight, and configurable desktop environment. The development of MX Respin ME stemmed from our playful experiment with one of the tools in MX Linux, MX Snapshot, a tool used for backing up the operating system as an ISO installer. Then, we thought, "What if we share this ISO with the public? Maybe someone would like to try it or might need it?" On the other hand, we also hope that MX Respin ME can become an option for those who need a free and legal computer operating system.

This computer operating system is also suitable for those who want to maximize the use of their desktops, laptops, and netbooks, offering a modern look and enough features for daily use.

Name and Icon

Initially, we named this operating system 'AI Project OS,' but we thought that was too much, so we went back to basics and named it MX Linux ME. After communicating with the lead developer of MX Linux, the operating system's name was changed again to what it is now, MX Respin ME, which means an operating system developed from MX Linux.

The MX Respin ME icon is inspired by three mountains in our area, known as the 3S (Mount Slamet, Sumbing, and Sindoro) among hikers. It also depicts a bird spreading its wings to fly. The mountains symbolize sturdiness, as we - Inshaa Allah - are steadfast in encouraging people to use creative works legally. The bird represents freedom, as people should be free to choose the creative works they use to meet their needs while respecting the applicable boundaries.

*It should be noted that the MX Respin ME computer operating system project is not directly connected to MX Linux, or it can be said that this MX Respin ME computer operating system project is a personal or community re-spin.

What makes MX respin ME?

Free Software
The MX respin ME project is committed to the principles of free software development; people are encouraged to use free software, improve it, and distribute it. Although it is not currently able to fully follow the principles of free software development. You can find out more about free software and the ideological and technical philosophies behind it at GNU website.

Linux
Linux was brought to life in 1991 by a Finnish student named Linus Torvalds. The Linux kernel is the heart of the MX Respin ME operating system. The kernel is an essential part of any operating system, providing a communication bridge between hardware and software. Read more about Linux at Linux kernel website.

XFCE
Xfce is a lightweight desktop environment used in MX Respin ME. It aims to be fast and low on system resources, while remaining visually appealing and user-friendly. Xfce embodies the traditional UNIX philosophy of modularity and reusability. Read more about Xfce at Xfce website.

MX Linux
MX Linux is a GNU/Linux-based operating system distribution developed by the Mepis and AntiX communities. It aims to produce an operating system that combines an elegant and efficient destop with high stability and solid performance. Read more about MX Linux at MX Linux website.

A Little Step

MX respin ME is a personally developed computer operating system, based on the MX Linux computer operating system. MX respin ME is an elegant and easy-to-use operating system. MX respin ME comes with Xfce, which is a stable, lightweight, and configurable desktop environment. The development of MX respin ME itself is intended for people to use a free and legal operating system as we use it, and for people to feel and enjoy what we feel and enjoy from this operating system.

This computer operating system is suitable for those who want to make the most of their desktops, laptops, and netbooks, featuring a modern look and enough features for efficient everyday use. It also works well on older hardware as well.

Name and Icon

At first we named this operating system 'AI Projek OS' but we thought it was too much, so we went back to square one and named it MX Linux ME. And after communicating with MX Linux developers, the name of this computer operating system was again changed to the current one, MX respin ME, which means an operating system developed from MX Linux.

The MX respin ME icon itself is inspired by the 3 mountains in our area and is known as 3S (Mount Slamet, Sumbing, and Sindoro) among hikers. It also depicts a bird flapping its wings to fly. The mountain symbolizes sturdiness as we -Insha Allah- invite people to use copyrighted works legally. The bird symbolizes freedom as people must be free in choosing the copyrighted works they want to use to help fulfill their needs while still paying attention to the applicable restrictions.

*It should be noted that this MX respin ME computer operating system project is not directly connected to MX Linux, or it can be said that this MX respin ME computer operating system project is a personal or community re-spin..

What makes MX respin ME?

Free Software
The MX respin ME project is committed to the principles of free software development; people are encouraged to use free software, improve it, and distribute it. Although it is not currently able to fully follow the principles of free software development. You can find out more about free software and the ideological and technical philosophies behind it at GNU website.

Linux
Linux was brought to life in 1991 by a Finnish student named Linus Torvalds. The Linux kernel is the heart of the MX Respin ME operating system. The kernel is an essential part of any operating system, providing a communication bridge between hardware and software. Read more about Linux at Linux kernel website.

XFCE
Xfce is a lightweight desktop environment used in MX Respin ME. It aims to be fast and low on system resources, while remaining visually appealing and user-friendly. Xfce embodies the traditional UNIX philosophy of modularity and reusability. Read more about Xfce at Xfce website.

MX Linux
MX Linux is a GNU/Linux-based operating system distribution developed by the Mepis and AntiX communities. It aims to produce an operating system that combines an elegant and efficient destop with high stability and solid performance. Read more about MX Linux at MX Linux website.

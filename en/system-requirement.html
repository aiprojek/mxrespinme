System Requirements

Ideal Specifications To Run MX respin ME

Minimum system requirements
To install and use MX respin ME 64-bit, you need an Intel or AMD 64-bit processor with at least 2 GB of memory (RAM). You can also try MX Re-spin ME with a USB or DVD with the same amount of memory. Keep in mind that running a system from a USB or DVD will be slower than an installed system.

When you install MX Re-spin ME, you need about 20 GB of free space on your hard disk and about 4 GB for LiveUSB.

Recommended system resources
To have a smooth experience when running multiple parallel applications on the desktop, it is recommended to have a 1.9Ghz Dual Core processor with at least 4 GB of memory.

It is recommended to have at least 30 GB of free space on your hard disk. This allows installation of new applications as well as storing your personal data on the hard disk in addition to the core system.
